Frequent asked Questions

What are the Seller App and Buyer App?

The Seller App is designed for retail vendors to manage their online store, list products, track sales, and connect with customers. The Buyer App allows customers to browse products, place orders, and enjoy a seamless shopping experience.

Are these apps free to use?

Yes, both the Seller App and Buyer App are free to download and use. However, there may be transaction fees or premium features in the future.

Where can I download the apps?

You can download the apps from the Google Play Store for Android and the Apple App Store for iOS.

How do I register as a seller?

Simply download the Seller App, sign up with your business details, verify your account, and start listing your products.

What types of businesses can use the Seller App?

ny retail vendor, small business, or entrepreneur looking to sell products online can use the Seller App.

How can I list my products?

Once registered, go to the product listing section, upload images, set prices, and provide product details.

How do I manage orders and payments?

The app provides a dashboard where you can track orders, manage inventory, and receive payments securely.

Are there any commissions or fees for selling on the app?

Currently, there are no listing fees, but transaction charges may apply based on payment gateways or future premium features.

What is the return or refund policy?

Returns and refunds depend on the seller's policy. You can check product details or contact the seller for more information.

Is my personal information safe?

Yes, we follow strict security measures to protect your data and ensure a secure shopping experience.
