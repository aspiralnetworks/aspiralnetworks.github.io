Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 28-03-2025

ASPIRAL NETWORKS PRIVATE LIMITED ("Company," "we," "our," or "us") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our products, YardStore and YardBuy, which include mobile applications and websites (collectively, the "Services").

By using our Services, you consent to the practices described in this policy. We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time, and your continued use of our Services signifies your acceptance of any changes.

  1. Information We Collect

    2. We collect different types of information based on your role as a buyer, seller, or user of our Services.

    1. Personal Information (For Buyers and Sellers)

      2. We may collect the following personal details when you register or interact with our Services:

      • Name, email address, phone number, and postal address
      • Login credentials and account security details
      • Payment details (processed securely through third-party payment gateways).
      • Government-issued identification details where necessary for verification.
      • For sellers, in addition to the general personal information collected, we may also collect business details, bank/payment account information, and order fulfillment records.
    2. Transaction and Usage Data
      • Order history, purchase records, and transaction details.
      • Seller business details, bank/payment account information, and order fulfillment records.
      • Technical data, including IP address, device type, browser information, and operating system.
      • Interaction logs, such as support requests and correspondence with our team.
    3. Location Data
      • We may collect and process location data if you allow location access while using our Services. This helps in optimizing delivery, seller discovery, and user experience.
    4. Non-Personal Information
      • Device information (such as IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers).
      • Usage data (such as browsing activity, interaction with features, and app performance metrics).
      • Location data (if you permit location services in our apps).
    5. Information from Third Parties
      • Data received from social media integrations (if you log in using social accounts).
      • Transaction history if purchases are made through partner merchants.
      • Seller ratings and performance data from users and internal analytics.
  2. Legal Basis for Processing Data

    3. We process personal data based on the following legal grounds:

    • Consent: When you explicitly agree to data collection and usage.
    • Contractual Necessity: To fulfill our obligations under user agreements.
    • Legal Compliance: To meet regulatory and legal requirements.

    • To process transactions, verify payments, and prevent fraud.
    • To facilitate seller-buyer interactions and order fulfillment.
    • To send you account-related notifications, updates, and security alerts.
    • To personalize content, recommendations, and marketing communications (with your consent).
    • Improve platform security, prevent fraud, and enforce our policies.
    • Send notifications regarding account activity, transactions, and updates.
    • To analyze user trends and enhance platform security.
    • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms & Conditions.
    • To conduct automated decision-making and profiling for fraud detection and personalized recommendations.

    • Sellers: Buyer contact details may be shared with sellers to facilitate order processing, fulfillment, and support.
    • We may engage third-party vendors for payment processing, identity verification, analytics, and customer support.
    • Legal Authorities: If required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our company, users, or others.
    • Corporate Transfers: In case of a business sale, merger, or acquisition, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction.
