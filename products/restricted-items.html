Restricted Items, Consequences, and a Summary of Terms & Conditions

To ensure a safe and lawful marketplace, the following items are strictly prohibited from being sold or bought on our platform. This list is not exhaustive and includes, but is not limited to:

1. Illegal & Controlled Substances

  • Narcotic drugs, psychotropic substances, and related paraphernalia
  • Alcohol, liquor, and tobacco products (where restricted by law)
  • Prescription medications, unapproved medical drugs, and steroids

Weapons & Hazardous Materials

  • Firearms, ammunition, explosives, and weapon accessories
  • Knives, swords, and weapons classified as illegal under Indian law
  • Hazardous chemicals, radioactive materials, or toxic substances

Counterfeit & Stolen Goods

  • Fake or replica branded products (luxury items, electronics, clothing, etc.)
  • Stolen goods or items acquired through illegal means

Digital & Financial Fraud Items

  • Unauthorized cryptocurrency sales or virtual assets not approved by Indian authorities
  • Stolen financial information (credit card data, bank accounts, etc.)
  • Hacking tools, spyware, malware, or digital piracy content

Offensive & Inappropriate Content

  • Pornographic materials, sexually explicit content, and adult services
  • Hate speech, discriminatory products, or materials promoting violence
  • Books, movies, or media banned under Indian law

Wildlife & Animal Products

  • Ivory, animal skins, or products made from endangered species
  • Live animals, exotic pets, or illegal wildlife trade

Government & Official Documents

  • Fake identification cards, passports, or government-issued documents
  • Military or police uniforms, badges, and insignia

Other Illegal Goods & Services

  • Any product or service that violates Indian laws and regulations
  • Any activity that promotes fraud, money laundering, or unethical business practices
  • Unauthorized lottery, gambling, betting, or investment schemes

Note: This list is not complete, and any item or service deemed illegal or inappropriate under Indian law may also be prohibited. We reserve the right to remove additional items that violate platform policies.

Consequences for Violating Restricted Items Policyg

  1. Removal of Listing – The prohibited item will be taken down without prior notice.
  2. Account Suspension or Ban – Users violating policies may face temporary or permanent suspension.
  3. Legal Action – Severe violations may be reported to law enforcement agencies.
  4. Financial Penalties – Fraudulent transactions or misuse may result in monetary fines.
  5. Loss of Seller Privileges – Violating sellers may be permanently banned from selling.
