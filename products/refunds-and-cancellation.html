Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated on 28-03-2025 13:04:36

At ASPIRAL NETWORKS PRIVATE LIMITED, we prioritize customer satisfaction and strive to provide a seamless experience. Our refund and cancellation policy is designed to be transparent and customer-friendly while ensuring fair business practices for all parties involved, including buyers, sellers, and ASPIRAL NETWORKS PRIVATE LIMITED as a technology platform.

  1. Order Cancellation
    • Customers may request cancellations within a time frame set by ASPIRAL NETWORKS PRIVATE LIMITED, which may be updated from time to time. This allows for accidental purchases to be rectified while ensuring sellers are not inconvenienced by last-minute cancellations.
    • If an order has already been accepted by the seller, the customer cannot cancel the order under any circumstances.
    • Sellers have the right to cancel orders at any time before they are marked as completed due to unavailability, operational constraints, or other valid reasons.
    • ASPIRAL NETWORKS PRIVATE LIMITED reserves the right to remove or modify the order cancellation feature from the customer application/website at any time without prior notice.
    • Exclusions: Orders containing perishable items such as flowers, food, or similar consumables cannot be canceled once placed. However, in cases where the delivered product is found to be defective or of poor quality, customers may be eligible for a refund or replacement (refer to the Refund Policy below).
  2. Refund Policy
    3. ASPIRAL NETWORKS PRIVATE LIMITED does not sell or buy products directly but provides a technology platform to connect buyers and sellers. Therefore, refunds are subject to the policies of the respective sellers. However, we facilitate the process by ensuring transparency and fair resolution. Refunds may be processed in the following cases:
    • Damaged or Defective Products: If a product is received in a damaged or defective condition, customers must report it to our Customer Service team within 30 minutes of delivery.
    • Mismatched Products: If the product received does not match the description or expectations, customers must raise a request within 30 minutes of receipt.
    • Order Non-Fulfillment: If a seller is unable to fulfill an order due to stock unavailability, logistical issues, or other unforeseen circumstances, customers will be entitled to a full refund.
    • Refund Processing Time: If a refund request is approved, it may take 16-30 business days for the amount to be credited back to the original payment method, depending on the banking and payment gateway processing times.
  3. Non-Refundable Situations
    • Customer Discretion: If the buyer changes their mind about a purchase after the order is accepted by the seller, no refunds will be provided.
    • Service Issues: ASPIRAL NETWORKS PRIVATE LIMITED does not take responsibility for product quality or service-related complaints as it is only a platform facilitating transactions. Customers are advised to resolve such disputes directly with the seller.
    • Manufacturer Warranty Claims: If a product is covered by a manufacturerâ€™s warranty, customers should contact the manufacturer directly for any issues instead of requesting a refund from the seller.
  4. Replacement and Exchange Policy
    • If a replacement is required due to a damaged or defective product, it must be reported within 30 minutes of order completion.
    • The replacement process may vary depending on product category, seller policies, and product availability.
    • Customers must return the product in its original packaging with all accessories and labels intact.
    • Exchanges are subject to approval by the seller, and ASPIRAL NETWORKS PRIVATE LIMITED will facilitate communication but does not guarantee an exchange outcome.
  5. Dispute Resolution Assistance
    • ASPIRAL NETWORKS PRIVATE LIMITED does not take direct responsibility for the resolution of disputes between buyers and sellers but provides assistance by collecting and sharing available transaction data such as order details, timestamps, and communication records.
    • Customers and sellers are expected to resolve disputes amicably based on the information provided by ASPIRAL NETWORKS PRIVATE LIMITED.
    • If a dispute arises regarding non-receipt, damaged goods, or incorrect items, we recommend that buyers first reach out to the seller directly via the platform.
    • If no resolution is reached, ASPIRAL NETWORKS PRIVATE LIMITED will provide mediation support but will not be responsible for enforcing refunds or compensations.
  6. Contact Information

    7. For any refund, cancellation, or dispute-related queries, please contact our Customer Service team using the details provided on our website or application.

    By placing an order with ASPIRAL NETWORKS PRIVATE LIMITED, you acknowledge and agree to abide by this Refund and Cancellation Policy.
