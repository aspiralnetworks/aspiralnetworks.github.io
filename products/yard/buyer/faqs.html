Buyer FAQs

Find answers to the most common questions about using the Yard Buyer App.

1. Getting Started

Q1. Who can register as a buyer on Yard?
Any individual or customer in India can register and start ordering products online.

Q2. How do I install the Yard Buyer App?
You can download the app from the Downloads page or directly from the Google Play Store / App Store.

Q3. Is there any registration fee?
No, registration is completely free. You only pay for the products you purchase.

2. Account & Verification

Q4. What information is required to create a buyer account?
Basic personal details such as name, mobile number, email, and delivery address are required.

Q5. How do I update my account information?
You can update your profile, contact details, and delivery addresses via the app settings.

Q6. Can my account be suspended?
Yes, for repeated policy violations, fraudulent activity, or misuse of the platform.

3. Orders & Delivery

Q7. How do I place an order?
Browse products, add to cart, select delivery address, and complete payment through the app.

Q8. How can I track my order?
Real-time order tracking is available in your dashboard within the app.

Q9. What if my order is delayed?
Contact support via the app or check the order status for updates. Delivery may depend on seller location and logistics partners.

4. Payments & Refunds

Q10. What payment methods are accepted?
Credit/Debit cards, UPI, net banking, and supported digital wallets.

Q11. Can I cancel an order?
Buyer-initiated cancellations are allowed before order confirmation. After confirmation, contact support for assistance.

Q12. How are refunds processed?
Refunds are processed via the original payment method within 5–7 business days.

5. Fees & Charges

Q13. Are there any platform fees?
Yes, a small service fee may be applied during checkout. All fees are clearly displayed before payment.

Q14. Are there hidden charges?
No, all charges are transparent and visible during the payment process.

6. Support & Contact

Q15. How do I contact support?
Use the Help & Support section within the app or email support@Aspiral Networks.com.

Q16. How can I report issues with a seller?
You can report problems via the order details page or contact support directly.
