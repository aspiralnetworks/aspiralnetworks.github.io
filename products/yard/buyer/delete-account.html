Account & Data Deletion — YardBuy (Buyer App)

This page explains how buyers can request deletion of their YardBuy account and associated personal data.

Operated by: Aspiral Networks Pvt. Ltd.

How to Delete Your Account (In-App)

  1. Open the YardBuy app.
  2. Go to Profile → My Profile.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Complete OTP verification.
  5. Confirm deletion.

This is the fastest and preferred method. You will receive a confirmation once the deletion is processed.

If You Cannot Access the App

Email us at: yard-services@spiralnetworks.in

Please include the following information:

  • Registered phone number or email
  • Full name
  • A statement requesting permanent deletion of your YardBuy account

We may verify your request via OTP or additional confirmation.

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Name
  • Phone number & email
  • Login credentials and authentication tokens
  • Saved addresses and delivery locations
  • App profile data, preferences, and saved items

What Data We May Retain

Certain information must be retained for legal, tax, and operational purposes, including:

  • Past orders
  • Payment confirmations and invoices
  • Refund records
  • Customer support communications related to transactions

All retained data is anonymized to remove personal identifiers and is kept only for lawful purposes.

Retention Period & Timeline

  • Acknowledgement of deletion request: 5 business days
  • Deletion processing: 30 days (except legally required records)
  • Statutory records (orders, invoices) may be retained up to 6 years per Indian law

Contact

If you have questions or need updates regarding your deletion request, contact: yard-services@spiralnetworks.in
