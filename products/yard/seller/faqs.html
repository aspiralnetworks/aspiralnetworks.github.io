Seller FAQs

Find answers to the most common questions about using the Yard Seller App.

1. Getting Started

Q1. Who can register as a seller on Yard?
Any Kirana store, local shop, or small/medium retailer in India can register and start selling online.

Q2. How do I install the Yard Seller App?
You can download the app from the Downloads page or directly from the Google Play Store.

Q3. Is there any onboarding fee?
No, onboarding is completely free. Only subscription plans are chargeable depending on your selection.

2. Verification & Account Setup

Q4. What documents are required for seller KYC?
Aadhar card, PAN card, and shop details are required for verification.

Q5. How long does seller/shop verification take?
Typically 24–48 hours after document submission.

Q6. Why was my KYC rejected?
Incorrect details, unclear images, or mismatched information can lead to rejection. You may resubmit updated documents.

3. Products & Listings

Q7. How do I add products?
Use the “Add Product” option, upload images, set pricing, and enter details. Our team reviews listings before publishing.

Q8. Why is my product pending approval?
All listings undergo manual review to ensure quality and safety. It usually takes up to 24 hours.

Q9. What kind of product images can I upload?
Clear product images, your own photos, or high-quality stock images you have rights to use.

Q10. Are there any restricted items?
Yes. Refer to the Restricted Items page for the full list.

4. Orders & Delivery

Q11. How will I receive new order notifications?
You receive in-app notifications and can view orders in your dashboard.

Q12. Who handles delivery?
Delivery options depend on the region. You may self-deliver or use logistics partners where available.

Q13. Can I cancel an order?
Sellers may cancel orders only under valid reasons, such as stock unavailability.

5. Payments & Withdrawals

Q14. How do I receive payments?
All payments are received online through secure gateway systems directly into your verified bank account.

Q15. When can I withdraw my earnings?
Anytime. Yard allows unlimited withdrawals after successful order completion.

Q16. Are there any withdrawal charges?
No. Yard does not charge any withdrawal fee. However, bank or payment gateway fees may apply.

6. Fees & Subscriptions

Q17. What fees do sellers pay?
Sellers pay only the subscription fee for the plan they choose.

Q18. Do buyers pay any fees?
Yes, a small platform fee is charged to buyers during checkout.

Q19. Are there hidden charges?
No. All fees are transparent and displayed clearly in the Pricing section.

7. Account & Support

Q20. Can I change my shop details?
Yes, you can update shop information in the settings menu (some changes require re-verification).

Q21. How do I contact support?
Use the “Support” section in the app or contact us through the email.

Q22. Can my seller account be suspended?
Yes, for repeated policy violations, restricted items, or fraudulent activity.
