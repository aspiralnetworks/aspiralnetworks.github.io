Restricted & Prohibited Items

To ensure safety, compliance, and a trustworthy marketplace, the following items cannot be listed or sold on Yard Seller App.

1. Illegal, Hazardous, or Regulated Items

The following items are strictly prohibited due to safety and legal regulations:

  • Drugs, narcotics, psychotropic, or controlled substances and related paraphernalia
  • Prescription medications, unapproved medical drugs, and steroids
  • Weapons, firearms, ammunition, explosives and weapon accessories
  • Knives, swords, and weapons classified as illegal under Indian law
  • Acids, chemicals, hazardous materials
  • Hazardous chemicals, radioactive materials, or toxic substances
  • Medical devices requiring a prescription
  • Counterfeit currency, items promoting illegal activity

2. Illegal or Counterfeit Products

Products violating trademark, copyright, or brand authenticity laws are not allowed.

  • Fake branded products
  • Replica electronics or accessories
  • Pirated CDs, DVDs, games, or software
  • Items using unauthorized brand logos
  • Stolen goods or items acquired through illegal means

3. Age-Restricted Items

Yard prohibits the sale of items requiring age verification.

  • Tobacco and tobacco-related products
  • Alcohol or alcoholic beverages
  • Pornographic materials, sexually explicit content, and adult services

4. Unsafe or Expired Items

To protect buyers, the following item types cannot be listed:

  • Expired food, medicines, cosmetics
  • Unpackaged or tampered consumables
  • Items failing safety, hygiene, or quality standards
  • Recalled or banned products

5. Items Promoting Violence or Harm

  • Hate speech materials
  • Discriminatory products, or materials promoting violence
  • Violent or extremist propaganda
  • Self-harm or suicide-related products
  • Books, movies, or media banned under Indian law

6. Other Restricted Categories

  • Human organs or biological materials
  • Animals or wildlife products
  • Lottery tickets, gambling products
  • Stolen goods or unverified items

7. Digital & Financial Fraud Items

  • Unauthorized cryptocurrency sales or virtual assets not approved by Indian authorities
  • Stolen financial information (credit card data, bank accounts, etc.)
  • Hacking tools, spyware, malware, or digital piracy content

8. Wildlife & Animal Products

  • Ivory, animal skins, or products made from endangered species
  • Live animals, exotic pets, or illegal wildlife trade

8. Government & Official Documents

  • Fake identification cards, passports, or government-issued documents
  • Military or police uniforms, badges, and insignia

8. Other Illegal Goods & Services

  • Any product or service that violates Indian laws and regulations
  • Any activity that promotes fraud, money laundering, or unethical business practices
  • Unauthorized lottery, gambling, betting, or investment schemes

Important Notice on Restricted Items

Sellers are strictly prohibited from listing any items mentioned in the Restricted & Prohibited Items list. All sellers must ensure that their products comply with applicable Indian laws and Yard Seller platform policies.

Violation of this policy may result in:

  • Immediate removal of the listed product
  • Temporary suspension or permanent termination of the seller account
  • Withholding of pending payouts in cases involving fraud or illegal products
  • Reporting to law enforcement authorities where legally required
  • Penalties as per applicable laws and Yard Seller platform terms

Yard Seller is committed to maintaining a safe and compliant marketplace. By using the platform, sellers agree to follow all listing policies without exception.

Important Notes

This list is not complete, and any item or service deemed illegal or inappropriate under Indian law may also be prohibited. We reserve the right to remove additional items that violate platform policies.

Yard Seller App reviews every product listing before it becomes public. Any listing found violating our policies will be removed, and repeated violations may result in account suspension.

For questions or clarifications, please contact our support team.
