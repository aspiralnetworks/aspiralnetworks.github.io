Subscription Plans

Yard Seller App offers flexible subscription plans designed to fit every size of retail business.

Gold Plan

For growing stores wanting more visibility and tools.

  • List up to 100 products

Platinum Plan

Full-featured tools for professional retailers.

  • Sales & performance reports

  • Priority Email support - Get response within 2 hours
₹500 / month

Platform & Transaction Fees

Yard keeps fees transparent and straightforward.

  • Platform Fee: Charged to buyers during checkout.
  • Seller Subscription: Monthly plan chosen by the seller.
  • Payment Gateway Charges: Standard UPI/card/bank fees apply.
