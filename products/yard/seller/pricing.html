Subscription Plans

Yard Seller App offers flexible subscription plans designed to fit every size of retail business.

Silver Plan

Basic plan.

  • List up to 50 products

  • Basic analytics
  • No featured placement
₹299 / month

Gold Plan

For growing businesses.

  • List up to 100 products
  • All features of SILVER plan
  • Featured in buyer search (priority placement)
  • Gold Email support - Get response within 6 hours
₹599 / month

Platinum Plan

For volume businesses.

  • List up to 1000 products
  • All features of GOLD plan

  • Early access to platform features
  • Email support - Get priority response within 2 hours
₹1199 / month

Platform & Transaction Fees

Yard keeps fees transparent and straightforward.

  • Platform Fee: Charged to buyers during checkout.
  • Seller Subscription: Monthly plan chosen by the seller.
  • Payment Gateway Charges: Standard UPI/card/bank fees apply.
