Account & Data Deletion – YardStore

How sellers can delete their YardStore account and personal data.

How to Delete Your Account (In-App)

  1. Open the YardStore app.
  2. Go to Profile → My Profile.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Verify using OTP and confirm the deletion.

This is the fastest and preferred method.

If You Cannot Access the App

Email us at: yard-services@spiralnetworks.in

Please include your:

  • Registered phone number or email
  • Your name
  • Statement requesting account deletion

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Name
  • Phone number & email
  • Login credentials
  • Profile details

Data We Must Retain

Some seller-related business and tax records must be kept for legal reasons, such as:

  • Order and transaction records
  • GST invoices & settlement references

All retained records are anonymized to remove your personal identity.

Retention Period

Required business and tax data may be retained for up to 6 years as per Indian law.

Contact

If you have questions or need updates regarding your deletion request, contact: yard-services@spiralnetworks.in
