Cancellation & Refund Policy

Applicable to All Buyers and Sellers Using the Yard Seller & Yard Buyer Apps and websites

Last Updated: 30-November-2025

1. INTRODUCTION

This Refunds & Cancellation Policy (“Policy”) governs how cancellations, refunds, returns, settlements, and payment reversals are handled on the Websites, Yard Buyer App and Yard Seller App (collectively, the “Platform”) operated by Aspiral Networks Private Limited (“Company”, “we”, “our”, “us”).

The Company:

  • Does not own, stock, sell, or supply products.
  • Acts solely as a technology platform connecting independent sellers with buyers.
  • Is not a party to any transaction between buyers and sellers.
  • Facilitates communication and payment processing but does not guarantee outcomes.

By using the Platform, buyers and sellers agree to the terms of this Policy.

2. SELLER SUBSCRIPTION FEES

(For Yard Seller App users)

  • All subscription fees (monthly/quarterly/annual) are non-refundable, irrespective of usage.
  • Cancellation of seller account does not entitle the seller to any refund.
  • The subscription remains active until the end of the paid billing cycle.

3. SELLER ACCOUNT CANCELLATION

Sellers may request account cancellation anytime. However:

  • No refunds will be issued for unused subscription periods.
  • All pending settlements must be completed before closure.
  • Certain transaction data may be retained as required by law (e.g., GST, transaction logs, payout history).

4. ORDER CANCELLATIONS (BUYERS & SELLERS)

4.1 Buyer-Initiated Cancellations (Yard Buyer App)

  • Buyers may cancel an order only before the seller accepts or ships it.
  • Once accepted/processed by the seller, order cancellation is not permitted.
  • Cancellation availability varies based on product category, logistics, and seller rules.
  • Non-cancellable items may include perishable goods and made-to-order items.

4.2 Seller-Initiated Cancellations (Yard Seller App)

Sellers may cancel orders before completion due to:

  • Out-of-stock items
  • Incorrect pricing
  • Operational issues
  • Fraud concerns
  • Listing or policy violations

4.3 Platform-Initiated Cancellations

The Company may cancel an order due to:

  • Suspected fraud
  • Restricted/prohibited items
  • Technical/System errors
  • Security or compliance concerns

The Company is not liable for losses arising from any form of cancellation, including loss of revenue, buyer dissatisfaction, or business impact.

5. REFUNDS TO BUYERS (SELLER RESPONSIBILITY)

Because the Platform is not the seller:

  • Sellers are fully responsible for refunding buyers for damaged, defective, incorrect, or undelivered products.
  • If a refund is approved, the Company may:
    • Deduct the refund amount from the seller’s Yard Wallet, OR
    • Adjust the amount in future settlements.

If a seller refuses or fails to refund, the Company may:

  • Hold seller payouts
  • Suspend or terminate the seller account
  • Report fraudulent behavior

6. BUYER REFUND PROCESS (Yard Buyer App)

Refunds may be processed in the following situations:

6.1 Cancelled Orders

  • Buyer-initiated or system-initiated cancellations result in refunds to the original payment method.

6.2 Returned Products

Refunds are approved only after:

  • Seller confirms receipt of the returned item
  • The product is validated as defective, damaged, or not as described

6.3 Processing Time

  • Refund timelines depend on payment gateway and bank processes.
  • The Company is not liable for delays caused by banks or UPI/payment gateways.

7. PRODUCT RETURNS (Buyers)

Returns are accepted only if:

  • The item is defective, damaged, or not as described
  • The buyer raises a return request within the allowed timeframe
  • The product is returned with all original packaging, tags, and accessories

Returns may be rejected if:

  • Damage is caused by customer misuse
  • Evidence (photo/video) is not provided
  • Return window has expired

8. SELLER WALLET & WITHDRAWALS

  • Withdrawal requests are processed within normal banking timelines.
  • Refunds to buyers for:
    • Cancelled orders
    • Disputes
    • Compliance violations
      • may be deducted from the seller’s wallet.

If deductions make the wallet balance negative:

  • The seller must clear dues within 7 days.
  • Failure to settle dues may result in account suspension.

9. INCORRECT SETTLEMENTS (Sellers)

Sellers must report settlement discrepancies within 5 working days. Corrections, will be reflected after verification.

10. PAYMENT GATEWAY FAILURES

The Company is not responsible for:

  • Banking delays
  • UPI failures
  • Reversal errors
  • Technical failures in third-party payment gateways

All settlement and refund timelines are governed by financial institutions.

11. FRAUD, MISUSE & POLICY VIOLATIONS

If fraudulent activity is detected, including but not limited to:

  • Fake orders or cancellations
  • Misrepresentation of products
  • Artificial inflation of sales
  • Refusal to process legitimate refunds
  • Selling prohibited or restricted items

The Company may:

  • Withhold or reverse payments
  • Suspend or terminate the seller account
  • Block buyer accounts
  • Take legal action where applicable

No refunds will be issued to fraudulent sellers or buyers.

12. NO LIABILITY STATEMENT

The Company is not liable for:

  • Product defects, damages, or quality issues
  • Losses due to cancellations, delays, or non-fulfillment
  • Disputes between buyers and sellers
  • Buyer dissatisfaction with product quality
  • Delays caused by third-party logistics or banks

Our role is strictly limited to facilitating transactions and payments.
