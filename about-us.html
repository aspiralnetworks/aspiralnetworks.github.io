About Aspiral Networks Pvt. Ltd.

Empowering India’s retail landscape through accessible, modern, and community-centric technology.

Our Story

Aspiral Networks Pvt. Ltd. was founded with a clear vision: to bring digital empowerment to India’s neighbourhood retailers. As the retail industry evolved rapidly, many small businesses struggled to keep pace with the growing dominance of large e-commerce platforms. We recognized that millions of local shops lacked visibility, digital tools, and the support required to compete in an increasingly online marketplace.

Driven by a commitment to uplift small retailers, we set out to develop technology that preserves the heart of Indian retail—trust, convenience, and personal relationships—while enabling businesses to thrive in the digital age.

What We Do

At Aspiral Networks, we build scalable digital solutions that simplify the everyday operations of small and medium retailers. Our technology is designed to bridge the gap between traditional commerce and the modern digital economy—without taking away what makes local retail special.

Our Flagship Product: Yard Services

Yard Services is a complete hyperlocal e-commerce ecosystem developed exclusively for Kirana stores and small retail businesses. With two mobile applications—one for sellers and one for buyers— our platform ensures smooth catalog management, secure payments, verified sellers, store KYC, bank verification, and fast order processing.

Retailers can upload products, manage inventory, track sales, withdraw earnings instantly, and monitor performance with real-time reports. Buyers enjoy an easy, trusted, and local shopping experience.
